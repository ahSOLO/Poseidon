Anyone can generate documents through Poseidon through three simple steps:

  1. Create a Template

    2. A document template is created in .DOCX format using your word processor of choice. Special tags are inserted into this document to prepare it for use as a template. Once created, the template can be uploaded onto Poseidon.


  2. Create a Form

    3. A unique form is created within Poseidon to match the template. This form sets out the type of data that you wish to incorporate into your template to generate the final document. The form can be shared, giving others the ability to generate documents based on your template.


  3. Populate the Form

    4. After the form is created, documents can be generated by filling out the form with the requested data. This data will be combined with the template into a new .DOCX file.


To begin the process, create a template.
